Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Tasneem application collects, uses, and protects your information.

Permissions

The Application may request access to your device’s location and notifications.

Location data may be used to provide location-based features and improve the service.

Notifications may be used to send updates, reminders, or important information.

Permissions are only requested with your consent and can be disabled at any time via device or app settings.

Information Collection

We may collect device information, usage data, and interaction data to improve the Application.

In-App Purchases

The Application uses in-app purchases to provide premium features.

All payments are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not directly collect or store your payment card information. Billing details are handled entirely by the respective app store provider.

Third-Party Services

The Application uses RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and purchases.

RevenueCat stores and processes purchase-related information such as subscription status, transaction receipts, and device identifiers to provide its services.

User Control

You can manage permissions, notifications, and subscriptions at any time through your device settings, app settings, or app store account.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data.
